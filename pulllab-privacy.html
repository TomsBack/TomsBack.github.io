PullLab Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

PullLab is a local-first pull-up tracking app. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data stored by the app

PullLab stores your pull-up baseline, workout sessions, set results, notes, and progress data locally on your device. This data is used to show your workout history and progress.

Accounts

PullLab does not require an account.

Data collection and sharing

The current version of PullLab does not send your workout data to our servers and does not sell or share your workout data with third parties.

Purchases

PullLab may offer optional Pro features in the future. If purchases are enabled, payments and subscription status may be processed by Google Play and RevenueCat. PullLab does not receive or store your full payment card details.

Ads and tracking

PullLab does not show ads and does not use advertising trackers.

Deleting your data

You can delete local PullLab data from the app's Settings screen by using Reset app. You can also remove local data by uninstalling the app.

Health and safety

PullLab is a fitness tracking tool, not medical advice. Stop training if you feel pain or dizziness and consult a qualified professional when needed.
